Final Project

For our final project my partner, Saida Fernandez, and I decided to create a variation of the classic Pac-Man game. Our game is called Snake Pac-Man. In our game Pac-Man doesnt go through a maze, instead the game is completely open like the snake game. The object of the game is to eat 15 lemons that appear randomly along the screen by hovering over them and clicking. But like Pac-Man our game has ghosts that you need to avoid, if they touch you then you lose your lives and eventually the game. And if you want to restart the game all you have to do is refresh the page.
